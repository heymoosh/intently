# Health Routine Strategy

**Type:** Strategy doc — why this belongs in the system at all

## Why This Project Exists

Energy is the constraint during a sprint, not time. When the workouts go, sleep degrades, focus degrades, mood follows. Tracking this as a project is the commitment device: it doesn't get quietly optimized away because "I'm busy."

The goal isn't performance. It's maintenance. Keep the baseline so everything else is possible.

## Current Approach

- 3x/week strength training: Monday, Wednesday, Friday
- 45-minute sessions, morning slot — committed to before any "I'll do it this evening" negotiation happens
- No tracking apps, no complicated programming — same 5-exercise rotation, progressive load over time

## Key Decisions

- 2026-04-01: Lock the schedule even during hackathon week. Protected like a calendar block, not a "when I have time" item.
- 2026-04-13: Recognized the skip-to-dip correlation from journal data — not just intuition. One missed session creates a two-day energy drag.

## Learnings

- 2026-04-13: Two missed sessions and the pattern starts to feel optional. The rule is: one slip is fine, two is a pattern starting.
