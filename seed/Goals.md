# Goals

## Vision (3-5 year horizon)

Ship something people rely on daily. Not "use sometimes" — actually rely on. Build it with someone good, not alone. Get to a point where the work funds itself and I have options. Stay strong and present for the people who matter. Make things that are worth making, even if just for me.

## Annual Milestones — 2026

- Intently: 10 real users by August
- 185 lbs by November (down from 194)
- Basement jam room setup complete
- At least one creative project shipped, even if tiny

## Goal Areas

### Career / Product
Build and ship a product with real traction. Find out if the builder path is sustainable. "Drift" looks like: chasing features instead of users, staying in build mode past the point of feedback, treating product work as identity without checking if anyone actually cares.

### Health
Consistent strength habit — not gym-culture performative, just functional. "Drift" looks like: workouts becoming "when I have time," sleep collapsing under a sprint, meals getting worse as focus gets better.

### Family
One dinner a week, present. Calls returned within 24 hours. "Drift" looks like: texting "busy this weekend" twice in a row.

### Creative
Music production on nights when the brain is done with structured work. Not a side hustle, not a pivot — just the thing that makes everything else make sense. "Drift" looks like: Ableton hasn't been opened in six weeks.
