# Runway Image Generation Prompts

**Style reference:** Upload style-reference-1.png and style-reference-2.png as style anchors for every prompt below.

> **NOTE:** Original style reference PNGs were lost when the hackathon folder was deleted. Re-upload the two reference images you sent in chat (woman at desk with phone, robot+person at laptops) — both are flat 2D vector illustrations with simplified character features, floating context icons, and warm-cool limited palette.

**Consistent style descriptor (append to every prompt):**
> Flat 2D vector illustration style. Clean geometric shapes, simplified character with expressive face, smooth rounded forms, limited warm-cool color palette (warm cream/butter yellow background, sage green, soft purple, terracotta accents). Floating context icons as scene props. White or warm cream background. Modern flat UI illustration, clean and minimal, professional but warm and approachable.

---

## Scene 1 — Calm / neutral starting state
A woman standing centered in a calm, slightly sparse warm cream space. Relaxed posture, soft smile, hands at sides. A few small floating app icons barely visible around her — calendar, phone, notes — small and unobtrusive. She looks at ease. Empty space around her feels peaceful, not lonely.

---

## Scene 2 — Rug pull / stumble
The same woman off-balance, one foot lifted, surprised expression — eyes wide, mouth slightly open. The ground beneath her looks unstable or has shifted. Her arms are out trying to catch herself. The previously calm space now feels slightly tilted.

---

## Scene 3 — The looking up moment (close crop)
Close crop on the woman's face and upper torso. Eyes wide and upward, expression of slow dawning realization — not panic, more like "oh no, when did this happen." Slightly furrowed brow. Warm cream background, nothing else in frame.

---

## Scene 5 — Tools appearing
The woman standing, looking around her with mild overwhelm as small floating app icons pop up one by one — calendar icon, email icon, phone icon, sticky note, reminder bell, todo list. Each icon has a soft drop shadow, floating at different heights around her. She's still upright, arms slightly raised.

---

## Scene 6 — Tornado / overwhelmed
The same woman with both hands pressing on her head, eyes squeezed shut or wide with stress. App icons and UI symbols (calendar, email, clock, notifications, todo lists) swirling in a spiral around her, overlapping and chaotic. Her hair is slightly disheveled. Expression is peak overwhelm.

---

## Scene 7 — Spinning into a dot
The woman spinning, body becoming a motion blur, shrinking toward the center of the frame. Swirling lines around her. She's becoming smaller — almost a dot. The surrounding chaos of icons is fading too.

---

## Scene 8 — The question mark
Wide open warm cream space. A single oversized question mark floating in the center, slightly above eye level. Clean, simple, bold. The emptiness around it is intentional — this is the only thing in frame.

---

## Scene 9 — Person reappears, thoughtful
The woman has reappeared, small in frame, standing below and looking up at the large floating question mark. Posture is contemplative — head slightly tilted, one hand near chin. Not scared, just thinking. Soft warm lighting.

---

## Scene 10 — Intently arrives
The woman and a glowing phone/app icon standing side by side, both facing the question mark together. The app icon has arrived from the right, now next to her. A sense of "we've got this." Warm sage green accent on the app icon.

---

## Scene 15 — Bright path appears
The woman standing on a path that stretches forward into the distance. The path is bright, straight, clear. At the very edges of the scene, the first hints of color and nature are bleeding in — a leaf, a small flower, a touch of green. She's facing forward.

---

## Scene 16 — World blooms
The woman walking forward confidently on her path. The world around her is fully alive — trees, birds in flight, flowers, warm golden light filtering through leaves. Sage greens, warm yellows, terracotta. The environment feels abundant and alive. She looks calm, purposeful, at home.

---

## Scene 17 — Final frame
The woman standing still, facing forward, looking ahead. Full nature environment surrounding her. Peaceful, certain, unhurried. This is the "I know where I'm going" moment. Warm, bright, full of life.
