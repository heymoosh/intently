# Runway Image Generation Prompts

**Style reference:** Upload style-reference-1.png and style-reference-2.png as style anchors for every prompt below.

**Consistent style descriptor (append to every prompt):**
> Flat 2D vector illustration style. Clean geometric shapes, simplified character with expressive face, smooth rounded forms, limited warm-cool color palette (sage green, soft purple, terracotta, butter yellow, peach accents). Floating context icons as scene props. **Background is specified per scene below — most scenes give an exact hex value to use as the dominant flat background fill; the final scene replaces the flat fill with a full natural environment.** Modern flat UI illustration, clean and minimal.

---

## Scene 1 — Neutral starting state
**Background color:** `#B8D9C0` (soft mint — flat fill, fills the frame)

A woman standing centered in a slightly sparse space. Neutral posture, neutral expression — not smiling, not frowning, just present and observing. Hands at sides. A few small floating app icons barely visible around her — calendar, phone, notes — small and unobtrusive. She is here to see, not at ease, not stressed. The empty space around her feels still and undecided — neither peaceful nor unsettled.

---

## Scene 2 — Rug pull / stumble
**Background color:** `#8E8FC6` (anxious dusk purple-blue — flat fill, fills the frame)

The same woman off-balance, one foot lifted, surprised expression — eyes wide, mouth slightly open. The ground beneath her looks unstable or has shifted. Her arms are out trying to catch herself. The space feels slightly tilted, the cool purple-blue background reinforcing unease.

---

## Scene 3 — The looking up moment (close crop)
**Background color:** `#8E8FC6` (anxious dusk purple-blue — flat fill, fills the frame)

Close crop on the woman's face and upper torso. Eyes wide and upward, expression of slow dawning realization — not panic, more like "oh no, when did this happen." Slightly furrowed brow. Flat dusk purple-blue background, nothing else in frame.

---

## Scene 5 — Tools appearing
**Background color:** `#C66B3F` (overwhelm terracotta clay — flat fill, fills the frame)

The woman standing, looking around her with mounting overwhelm as small floating app icons pop up one by one — calendar icon, email icon, phone icon, sticky note, reminder bell, todo list. Each icon has a soft drop shadow, floating at different heights around her. She's still upright, arms slightly raised. The hot terracotta background presses in.

---

## Scene 6 — Tornado / overwhelmed
**Background color:** `#C66B3F` (overwhelm terracotta clay — flat fill, fills the frame)

The same woman with both hands pressing on her head, eyes squeezed shut or wide with stress. App icons and UI symbols (calendar, email, clock, notifications, todo lists) swirling in a spiral around her, overlapping and chaotic. Her hair is slightly disheveled. Expression is peak overwhelm. The hot terracotta background fully saturates the scene.

---

## Scene 7 — Spinning into a dot
**Background color:** `#C66B3F` (overwhelm terracotta clay — flat fill, fills the frame)

The woman spinning, body becoming a motion blur, shrinking toward the center of the frame. Swirling lines around her. She's becoming smaller — almost a dot. The surrounding chaos of icons is fading too. The terracotta background still dominates the frame.

---

## Scene 8 — The question mark
**Background color:** `#CFC9EB` (transitional soft lilac — flat fill, fills the frame)

Wide open lilac space — the heat has drained out of the scene but it isn't calm yet. A single oversized question mark floating in the center, slightly above eye level. Clean, simple, bold. The emptiness around it is intentional — this is the only thing in frame.

---

## Scene 9 — Person reappears, thoughtful
**Background color:** `#CFC9EB` (transitional soft lilac — flat fill, fills the frame)

The woman has reappeared, small in frame, standing below and looking up at the large floating question mark. Posture is contemplative — head slightly tilted, one hand near chin. Not scared, just thinking. Soft lilac background — quieter than the previous scenes but not yet warm.

---

## Scene 10 — Intently arrives
**Background color:** `#F5EEE0` (warm linen — flat fill, fills the frame; this is Intently's actual app surface color, so the world adopts the app's palette the moment it arrives)

The woman and a glowing phone/app icon standing side by side, both facing the question mark together. The app icon has arrived from the right, now next to her. A sense of "we've got this." The background warms to linen as the app arrives — the first truly calm and warm note in the sequence. Sage green accent on the app icon.

---

## Scene 15 — Bright path appears (Whorl blooms)
**Background color:** `#F1DE8A` (warmest butter yellow — flat fill, fills the frame; this is the warmth peak of the sequence)

The woman standing on a path that stretches forward into the distance. The path is bright, straight, clear. At the very edges of the scene, the first hints of color and nature are bleeding in — a leaf, a small flower, a touch of green. She's facing forward. The butter-yellow background glows like sunlight — this is the bloom moment.

---

## Scene 16 — World blooms
**Background color:** `#F1DE8A` (butter yellow — flat fill, fills the frame; same as scene 15 to hold the warmth peak across both)

The woman walking forward confidently on her path. The world around her is fully alive — trees, birds in flight, flowers, warm golden light filtering through leaves. Sage greens, peach, terracotta accents on the foliage against the butter-yellow background. The environment feels abundant and alive. She looks calm, purposeful, at home.

---

## Scene 17 — Final frame
**Background:** No flat color fill. The natural environment IS the background — leaves, foliage, sky, trees, soft golden light. Render the full nature scene edge-to-edge so there is no flat hex backdrop showing through.

The woman standing still, facing forward, looking ahead. She is fully inside a lush natural environment — trees, leaves, flowers, soft sunlight filtering through foliage, gentle sky tones at the top of frame. Peaceful, certain, unhurried. This is the "I know where I'm going" moment. She is in nature, not in front of it — the environment fills every part of the frame.
